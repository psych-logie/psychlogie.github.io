About Us

DR Vijayalakshmi

M.Sc Psychology | M.Phil Clinical Psychology | Ph.D. Psychology | Certified Child Counselor | Cognitive Behavior Therapist | Behavior and Occupational Therapy Specialist

With over 15 years of experience in the field of psychology, DR Vijayalakshmi is dedicated to helping individuals and families achieve mental well-being and personal growth. Her approach is compassionate, evidence-based, and tailored to meet the unique needs of each client.

What We Do

At Psych Logie, we provide a wide range of psychological services, including:

  • Therapy Consultations: Individual therapy, Cognitive Behavior Therapy (CBT), and Behavior and Occupational Therapy for all ages.
  • Stress Management: Counseling for anxiety, depression, and work-life balance.
  • Family Counseling: Improving communication and resolving conflicts within families.
  • Child Behavior Counseling: Support for children with behavioral issues, emotional regulation, and developmental delays.
  • Academic Support: Assistance with thesis writing, research proposals, and data analysis.
